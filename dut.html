e-Flooding

TAG Internship

I did a 3-month internship in a research team at IRIT on the project i-Nondations (e-flooding). It aims to handle fast floods to improve crisis management and resiliency. My aim was to prioritize the issues at risk during floods to enable an existing crisis management system to optimize rescue missions during flash floods. I had to prioritize the stakes under the influence of water according to their characteristics, in order to determine an optimal route for the rescue vehicles. I used Python and QGIS via a Linux Ubuntu environment.
